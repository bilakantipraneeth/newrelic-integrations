## 🧠 Master Prompt Template for Deep Learning

*"Act as an expert technical instructor and senior engineering practitioner. I want to deeply study and master **[Insert Topic Here]**. Please structure my learning path with the following comprehensive breakdown:*

1. **Core Fundamentals:** *What are the absolute essential concepts, definitions, and mental models I must understand before touching any tools or code?*
2. **Real-World Application:** *Provide 2-3 practical case studies or real-world scenarios showing how this technology solves actual engineering problems.*
3. **Hands-On Exercises:** *Give me 3 progressively difficult, step-by-step practical exercises I can execute locally or in a free tier environment.*
4. **Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns:** *What are the most common mistakes, misconceptions, and bad practices beginners fall into, and how can I avoid them?*
5. **Curated Resources:** *Recommend the definitive documentation pages, specific articles, high-quality YouTube channels, and essential books for deeper mastery.*
6. **Structured 4-Week Mastery Plan:** *Create a week-by-week study plan with clear milestones and checkpoints to measure my progress."*
